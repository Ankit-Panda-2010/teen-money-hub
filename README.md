# Teen Money Hub

A simple and fun money management app designed specifically for high school students to learn about personal finance and build smart money habits.

## 🎯 Made for Teens

This app helps high school students:
- Track spending on things they actually buy
- Set savings goals for stuff they want
- Learn money skills that matter now
- Plan for bigger purchases like a car or college

## Features

### 📊 Dashboard
- **Total Money**: See what you have right now
- **Money In**: Track allowance, job earnings, birthday money
- **Spending**: See where your money goes each month
- **Money Saved**: How much you're keeping for future goals

### � Spending Tracker
- **Add What You Bought**: Quick entry for purchases
- **Teen Categories**: Food & Snacks, Getting Around, Fun & Games, Shopping, Friends & Gifts
- **Recent Spending**: See your last purchases
- **Easy Delete**: Remove mistakes easily

### 💰 Allowance & Spending Limits
- **Set Limits**: Decide how much to spend in each category
- **Visual Progress**: See how close you are to your limits
- **Smart Alerts**: Know when you're getting close to overspending

### � Savings Goals
- **What Are You Saving For?**: Phone, car, college, games, clothes
- **Track Progress**: See how close you are to your goal
- **Add Money**: Update your savings as you add more
- **Visual Motivation**: Progress bars keep you motivated

### 💡 Money Tips
- **Daily Tips**: New money wisdom every day
- **6 Categories**: Saving Money, Earning Money, Smart Spending, Future Planning, Money Skills, Avoiding Debt
- **Teen-Focused**: Real advice for your life right now
- **Search & Filter**: Find exactly what you need

### 🧮 Money Calculators
- **Savings Goal**: How long to save for something you want
- **Weekly Allowance**: See how much you could save
- **Job Money**: Calculate earnings from part-time work

## 🎨 Why Teens Love It

- **Simple Language**: No confusing financial terms
- **Relatable Examples**: Actually about things teens buy and save for
- **Fun Design**: Bright colors and easy-to-use interface
- **No Judgment**: Learn at your own pace
- **Real Skills**: Stuff you'll actually use

## 📱 How to Use

1. **Open the app** - No installation needed!
2. **Set your monthly income** - Allowance, job money, etc.
3. **Add your spending** - Track what you buy
4. **Set savings goals** - Plan for big purchases
5. **Learn money tips** - Get smarter about money

## 💡 Smart Money Tips Included

- Save 10% of everything you get
- The 50/30/20 rule for teens
- Wait 24 hours before big purchases
- Find student discounts everywhere
- Start small businesses (babysitting, lawn mowing)
- Compare prices before buying
- Pack lunch instead of buying school lunch

## 🔧 Technology
